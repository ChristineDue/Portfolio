Dream your portfolio

The assignment was to make a prototype of your own portfolio. I made the prototype in Adobe XD and below here, you can see the process:

Adobe XD is an effective way to see process. It is a quick tool to make a tempplate of, how you want your final project to look like.
I startet out with a littele research, and found out, that i would use the method "mobile first". Mobile first means, that most things are being created with mobile in mind. Mobile devices is our way to stay connected and our way to communicate.

Because of the "mobile first"-term, I used UI kits to make the prototype more authentic.
Part of the prototyping is also userbility-test. While I've tested my prototype on friends and family I found out witch of the things that worked and witch that did not.
